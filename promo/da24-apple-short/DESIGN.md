# DA24 Apple Short Promo

## Style Prompt

Premium Apple-inspired Korean home-service promo for a 9:16 short. Use a clean glassy white canvas, calm system-blue accents, graphite text, rounded cards, soft depth, and confident product typography. The piece should feel like a new DA24 brand reveal, not a copied service marketplace page.

## Colors

- Background: `#F5F5F7`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Foreground: `#1D1D1F`
- Secondary text: `#515154`
- Muted text: `#86868B`
- Accent blue: `#0071E3`
- Accent green: `#34C759`
- Graphite: `#2C2C2E`

## Typography

- Primary: `-apple-system`, `BlinkMacSystemFont`, `SF Pro Display`, `Apple SD Gothic Neo`, `Pretendard`, sans-serif
- Headlines use 800-900 weight, tight tracking, large video-safe sizes.
- Body uses 400-600 weight, generous line-height.

## Motion

- Calm premium motion: blur crossfades, soft vertical entrances, gentle scale, polished card stagger.
- Use transitions between scenes, no jump cuts.
- Keep the final CTA clear for at least two seconds.

## What NOT to Do

- No old DA24 logo treatment or original site-like blue mobile clone.
- No dark neon, bokeh blobs, or noisy ad style.
- No tiny web UI text that cannot be read in a short.
- No forced dense feature lists.
